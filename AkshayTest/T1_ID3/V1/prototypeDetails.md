# Marketing Brief

---

## Target Persona

Health-conscious adults seeking convenient, high-protein breakfast options that fit busy lifestyles and prioritize both nutrition and taste.

---

## Value Proposition

Protein-Boosted Cereal Packs offer single-serve convenience with added plant-based protein and dried fruit, specifically designed to meet the protein and fiber needs of adults. Each pack delivers a balanced, nutrient-dense breakfast solution that is easy to enjoy at home or on the go. The product leverages existing cereal production capabilities, ensuring low incremental cost and operational feasibility. By focusing on adult nutritional gaps, this solution supports a healthier start to the day without sacrificing flavor or convenience.

---

## Business Impact

This innovation drives incremental revenue by targeting adult consumers seeking functional breakfast options, expanding the brand’s reach beyond traditional cereal buyers. It leverages efficient production and addresses a clear market need, supporting both volume growth and brand relevance.
