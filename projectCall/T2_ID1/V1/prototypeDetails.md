# Marketing Brief

---

## Target Persona

Project creators and users within General Mills’ digital platforms who require immediate, in-flow assistance during project setup or management—especially those handling complex or high-touch support needs.

---

## Value Proposition

This solution embeds a real-time chat support interface directly into the project creation workflow, connecting users instantly to support agents or AI chatbots. Users receive immediate, context-aware assistance without leaving their task, reducing friction and confusion. The system is designed for seamless integration, ensuring support is always accessible at the exact moment of need. This approach directly addresses high-touch support requirements, improves user satisfaction, and minimizes operational delays, all while maintaining moderate operational costs and leveraging scalable digital support infrastructure.

---

## Business Impact

By providing instant, embedded support, this integration reduces project setup errors, accelerates user onboarding, and increases satisfaction. The result is higher retention, fewer support tickets, and a measurable boost in workflow efficiency and user confidence.
