# Marketing Brief

---

## Target Persona

Parents of infants and toddlers (ages 6–24 months) seeking convenient, nutritious, and engaging snack options for their babies, with a preference for direct-to-consumer delivery and interactive, social media–friendly experiences.

---

## Value Proposition

Churios Subscription Boxes deliver a curated monthly assortment of Churios cereal varieties directly to parents' homes via a dedicated DTC website. Each box is designed for babies and toddlers, featuring age-appropriate flavors and textures. The subscription includes social media integration, encouraging parents to share unboxing moments and feedback, fostering a community of engaged caregivers. This approach supports the goal of increasing focus on babies by making nutritious snacking convenient, interactive, and tailored to early childhood needs, while leveraging digital engagement to amplify reach and collect actionable insights.

---

## Business Impact

This initiative drives incremental revenue through recurring DTC sales, deepens brand loyalty among young families, and generates valuable user-generated content for marketing. It also strengthens General Mills' position in the baby snack segment by directly engaging a high-potential, retention-prone audience.
