# Marketing Brief

---

## Target Persona

Parents of babies and toddlers seeking convenient, fun, and varied snack options delivered directly to their homes.

---

## Value Proposition

General Mills offers a monthly Churios variety subscription box, delivered via a dedicated DTC website. Each box features a curated selection of Churios flavors, designed for easy discovery and snack-time excitement. Social media integration encourages parents to share unboxing moments and provide feedback, creating a playful, interactive experience. The service is tailored for families with young children, making snack time both convenient and engaging, while supporting the goal of increasing focus on babies.

---

## Business Impact

This subscription model drives recurring revenue, deepens brand engagement with young families, and leverages social media to amplify reach and gather real-time feedback, supporting growth in the baby and toddler segment.
