# Marketing Brief

---

## Target Persona

Parents of school-aged children (ages 6–12) who enjoy hands-on family activities and are interested in creative, at-home snack experiences that combine convenience with interactive fun.

---

## Value Proposition

Churios DIY Snack Kits provide families with all the ingredients and step-by-step instructions needed to create unique, Churios-based snacks at home. Each limited-edition kit is designed for easy unboxing and includes recipe tutorials accessible via QR code, encouraging shared family moments and creativity. The kits are promoted through engaging unboxing content and influencer-led recipe demonstrations, making snack time both interactive and memorable. This approach leverages hands-on trends and supports moderate development and packaging costs, ensuring operational feasibility and a differentiated product experience.

---

## Business Impact

The Churios DIY Snack Kits drive incremental revenue by tapping into the growing demand for experiential, at-home food activities. The kits generate social buzz through unboxing and tutorial content, amplifying brand engagement and encouraging repeat purchases, while maintaining manageable development and packaging costs.
