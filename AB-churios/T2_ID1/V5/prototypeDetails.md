# Marketing Brief

---

## Target Persona

Families with children aged 6–14 who enjoy hands-on kitchen activities and are active on social media platforms, especially those seeking creative, interactive snack experiences at home.

---

## Value Proposition

Churios DIY Snack Kits provide families with all the ingredients and step-by-step instructions needed to create unique Churios-based snacks at home. Each kit is designed for easy unboxing and includes recipe tutorials accessible via QR code, encouraging shared family experiences and creativity. The limited-edition nature of the kits drives urgency and collectability, while the unboxing and recipe content is optimized for social sharing. This approach leverages hands-on trends and supports moderate development and packaging costs, ensuring operational feasibility and alignment with current consumer engagement behaviors.

---

## Business Impact

The Churios DIY Snack Kits generate incremental revenue by tapping into the experiential snacking trend, increasing brand engagement and household penetration. The kits drive demand through limited-time offers and social media amplification, supporting both short-term sales spikes and long-term brand affinity.
