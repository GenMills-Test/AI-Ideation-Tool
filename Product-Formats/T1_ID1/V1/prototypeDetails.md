# Marketing Brief

---

## Target Persona

Urban and suburban grocery shoppers who frequently purchase snacks and cereals, are price-sensitive, and respond to real-time deals, especially during local events or peak shopping hours.

---

## Value Proposition

General Mills introduces an AI-driven dynamic pricing engine for granola bars, parfait mixes, and cereals. This system automatically adjusts prices in real time based on demand, time of day, and local events, ensuring shoppers receive competitive deals while maximizing margin during high-traffic periods. The solution integrates seamlessly with retail partners, leveraging live data feeds and event calendars. Execution is focused on operational efficiency and margin optimization, with ongoing data management and moderate tech investment required to maintain responsiveness and accuracy.

---

## Business Impact

This initiative drives incremental revenue by capturing peak demand opportunities, optimizes margin through intelligent price adjustments, and strengthens retailer partnerships by delivering value to both shoppers and stores. Operational feasibility is balanced with high impact potential.
