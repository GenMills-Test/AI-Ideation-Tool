# Marketing Brief

---

## Target Persona

Eco-conscious Gen Z consumers seeking sustainable, low-sugar and low-calorie snack options that align with their values and lifestyle. Primarily urban dwellers who prioritize environmental impact and ingredient transparency in their food choices.

---

## Value Proposition

We offer innovative snacks crafted from upcycled grains and fruit pulp, delivering delicious flavor and satisfying crunch while minimizing food waste and added sugar. Each snack is thoughtfully produced to highlight sustainability, using ingredients that would otherwise be discarded. This approach not only reduces environmental impact but also appeals to Gen Z’s demand for transparency and responsible sourcing. The product is designed for easy integration into daily routines, supporting mindful snacking without compromising on taste or values.

---

## Business Impact

This initiative drives incremental growth by tapping into the sustainability-driven snacking segment, enhancing brand reputation among Gen Z, and reducing ingredient costs through upcycling. It supports long-term loyalty and positions the brand as a leader in clean label innovation.
