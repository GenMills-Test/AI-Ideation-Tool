# Marketing Brief

---

## Target Persona

Snack enthusiasts and food trend seekers attending public events in urban and suburban areas, interested in interactive tasting experiences and influencing new product launches.

---

## Value Proposition

General Mills will host a National Tasting Tour featuring pop-up kiosks at high-traffic events, where attendees can sample finalist snack flavors and cast secure digital votes. Each kiosk is designed for quick, hygienic sampling and real-time voting, with live results displayed to engage participants. This approach ensures direct consumer input on new product development, aligns with retail compliance requirements, and leverages experiential trends to create memorable brand interactions. The execution is focused on operational efficiency, robust digital infrastructure, and seamless event logistics to maximize participation and data integrity.

---

## Business Impact

This initiative drives demand generation by directly engaging target consumers, capturing actionable flavor preferences, and amplifying brand presence through experiential marketing. Real-time voting data informs product launches, supporting higher retail acceptance and reducing go-to-market risk.
