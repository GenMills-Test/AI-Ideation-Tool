# Marketing Brief

---

## Target Persona

Families and football fans who enjoy exclusive, limited-time snacks and collectible packaging, especially those hosting or attending Super Bowl gatherings and seeking unique treats to enhance their game day experience.

---

## Value Proposition

The Super Bowl Cookie Mystery Box offers a limited-edition assortment of surprise cookie flavors in collectible packaging, available exclusively during Super Bowl week. Each box contains a mix of unique, never-before-seen cookies, designed to create excitement and anticipation for families and football fans. The collectible packaging adds a keepsake element, driving FOMO and encouraging immediate purchase. The offering is cost-effective, leveraging existing production capabilities while introducing novelty through flavor and design rotation. This approach maximizes engagement and supports operational efficiency, aligning with the need for exclusivity and timely execution.

---

## Business Impact

This initiative drives incremental revenue by capitalizing on Super Bowl demand spikes, leveraging exclusivity and collectible appeal to boost impulse purchases. It amplifies brand engagement, encourages social sharing, and increases basket size during a high-traffic retail period.
