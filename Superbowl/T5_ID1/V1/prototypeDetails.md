# Marketing Brief

---

## Target Persona

Superbowl viewers aged 18–45 who value convenience and crave fresh snacks delivered instantly to their homes and offices during live sports events, especially those hosting or attending game day gatherings.

---

## Value Proposition

Pillsbury partners with leading delivery apps to offer instant, freshly baked cookie delivery during the Superbowl. Customers can order exclusive game day bundles featuring warm cookies and receive flash discounts throughout the event. The service is designed for groups who want to enjoy premium snacks without leaving the action, ensuring cookies arrive hot and ready to share. Execution leverages real-time app integration and targeted Superbowl promotions, aligning with convenience-driven consumption and operational feasibility.

---

## Business Impact

This initiative drives incremental revenue by capturing in-the-moment demand during the Superbowl, amplifies brand presence through exclusive digital partnerships, and increases product trial and repeat purchase by making Pillsbury cookies the go-to snack for live sports occasions.
