# Marketing Brief

---

## Target Persona

Affluent, trend-conscious Gen Z and Millennial consumers in premium international markets who seek unique, high-quality chocolate and coconut products as exotic imports, typically shopping at upscale grocery and specialty food retailers.

---

## Value Proposition

General Mills will establish export partnerships with select premium international retailers to position its chocolate and coconut products as exclusive, high-quality imports. These products will feature elegant packaging and emphasize their exotic origins, appealing to consumers who value global flavors and premium experiences. The offering is tailored for markets where imported specialty foods are seen as status symbols, and the execution will focus on maintaining product freshness, visual appeal, and consistent supply. The approach leverages General Mills’ global brand reputation and operational scale to ensure reliable delivery and strong in-store presence, while aligning with the expectations of discerning, trend-driven shoppers.

---

## Business Impact

This initiative will drive incremental revenue by capturing high-margin export opportunities, enhance brand prestige in international markets, and create new demand among consumers seeking premium, globally sourced treats. It supports global expansion with moderate export costs and scalable distribution.
