# Marketing Brief

---

## Target Persona

Tech-savvy snack enthusiasts aged 18–35 who frequently purchase snacks, engage with brands online, and seek interactive, rewarding digital experiences tailored to their preferences.

---

## Value Proposition

General Mills introduces a mobile Snack Loyalty App where users earn points for every purchase, review, and social share. The app delivers personalized challenges based on individual purchase history, driving ongoing engagement. Points are redeemable for exclusive snacks or unique experiences, creating a dynamic, gamified ecosystem. This approach leverages digital engagement to foster brand loyalty, encourages repeat purchases, and amplifies word-of-mouth through social sharing. The execution is designed for seamless integration with existing retail and digital channels, ensuring operational feasibility and maximizing user participation.

---

## Business Impact

The Snack Loyalty App will drive incremental sales by incentivizing repeat purchases and social advocacy, deepen customer insights through data-driven personalization, and strengthen brand loyalty among digitally engaged consumers.
