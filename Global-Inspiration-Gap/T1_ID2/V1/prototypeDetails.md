# Marketing Brief

---

## Target Persona

Gen Z snackers and young adults seeking adventurous, globally inspired flavors in convenient, portable formats—especially those drawn to street food experiences and bold taste combinations.

---

## Value Proposition

Nature Valley Latin Street Corn Snack Bars offer a unique fusion of roasted corn, cotija cheese, lime, and chili, capturing the vibrant essence of Mexican elote in a convenient bar. Designed for on-the-go consumers who crave bold, authentic flavors, these bars deliver a satisfying, savory snack experience. The product leverages scalable production and moderate development costs, ensuring operational feasibility while meeting the demand for innovative, globally inspired snacks. Execution focuses on maintaining ingredient authenticity and consistent quality, aligning with Gen Z’s desire for new taste adventures and street food culture.

---

## Business Impact

This launch expands Nature Valley’s portfolio into global flavor fusions, driving incremental demand among younger consumers. It supports brand relevance, increases shelf differentiation, and leverages operational scalability for efficient nationwide rollout, fueling both trial and repeat purchases.
