# Marketing Brief

---

## Target Persona

Socially-driven Gen Z snackers seeking unique, shareable food experiences for solo enjoyment or group occasions.

---

## Value Proposition

The Multistage Flavor Experience Bar Set offers a boxed collection of snack bars, each crafted with sequential layers of dark chocolate, strawberry, and lemon. Designed for guided flavor progression, these bars deliver a memorable tasting journey—whether enjoyed alone or as part of a social event. The product’s elegant packaging and engineered layering cater to Gen Z’s desire for experiential, Instagram-worthy snacks, supporting both personal indulgence and group sharing. Execution focuses on precise flavor sequencing and premium presentation, aligning with clear success metrics and moderate investment requirements.

---

## Business Impact

This concept drives incremental demand by tapping into experiential snacking and social sharing trends, increasing brand relevance among Gen Z. The unique format supports gifting, group occasions, and repeat purchases, amplifying both trial and retention.
