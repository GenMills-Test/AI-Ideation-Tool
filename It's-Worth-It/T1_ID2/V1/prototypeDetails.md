# Marketing Brief

---

## Target Persona

Environmentally conscious chocolate consumers aged 25–45 who prioritize sustainability transparency, seek premium indulgence products, and actively verify ethical sourcing claims through digital tools before purchase decisions.

---

## Value Proposition

This initiative produces chocolate bars in manufacturing facilities powered entirely by renewable solar energy infrastructure. Each bar features a batch-specific QR code printed on packaging that enables consumers to access real-time verification of sustainability credentials including solar energy usage, production date, facility location, and carbon offset metrics. The system bridges the gap between sustainability claims and consumer trust by providing transparent, traceable proof of renewable production at the individual product level. Execution requires phased solar installation across production sites and integration of batch-tracking software with consumer-facing verification platforms.

---

## Business Impact

Drives premium positioning and price justification through verified sustainability differentiation in the competitive chocolate category. Creates measurable consumer engagement via QR scan analytics, enabling direct-to-consumer data capture and retention pathways. Supports multi-brand portfolio application across chocolate and confectionery lines, establishing General Mills as a category leader in transparent sustainable manufacturing while meeting rising retailer and regulatory sustainability disclosure requirements.
