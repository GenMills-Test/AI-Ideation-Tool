# Marketing Brief

---

## Target Persona

Gen Z online snack shoppers seeking personalized, health-conscious snack options that fit their dietary preferences and digital lifestyles.

---

## Value Proposition

A virtual nutritionist chatbot is integrated directly into e-commerce and brand sites, analyzing user dietary profiles to recommend potato chips as a healthy snack option. This solution leverages digital engagement to provide tailored, real-time snack suggestions, making it easy for Gen Z consumers to discover better-for-you chips that align with their health goals. The chatbot ensures recommendations are retail-compliant and seamlessly fits into the online shopping journey, supporting smarter snacking decisions without adding friction.

---

## Business Impact

Drives incremental demand for potato chips by converting health-focused Gen Z shoppers through personalized recommendations, increasing basket size and brand loyalty while maintaining compliance with retail standards and leveraging digital health engagement trends.
