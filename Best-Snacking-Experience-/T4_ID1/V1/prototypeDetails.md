# Marketing Brief

---

## Target Persona

Online cereal shoppers—specifically convenience-focused families and cereal enthusiasts seeking variety, exclusive products, and direct delivery options.

---

## Value Proposition

General Mills will launch a branded direct-to-consumer online store for cereals, offering exclusive products, curated bundles, and flexible subscription options. This platform bypasses traditional retail limitations, providing customers with access to limited-edition cereals, personalized bundles, and recurring delivery. The store is designed for digital-savvy consumers who value convenience, variety, and direct engagement with the brand. Execution will focus on seamless e-commerce experience, reliable fulfillment, and targeted promotions to drive adoption and retention.

---

## Business Impact

The online store creates a new revenue stream by capturing digital-first shoppers, increasing brand loyalty, and enabling direct consumer relationships. It amplifies distribution reach, supports demand generation, and provides valuable data for future product innovation.
