# Marketing Brief

---

## Target Persona

Busy families and working professionals who regularly consume breakfast cereals and value convenience, cost savings, and reliable home delivery.

---

## Value Proposition

General Mills offers a subscription-based discount model for its cereal range, enabling customers to schedule regular home deliveries at a locked-in, reduced price. This service ensures that households never run out of their favorite cereals, streamlining breakfast routines and providing predictable savings. The model is designed for ease of use, with flexible delivery intervals and simple online management, directly addressing the needs of time-pressed consumers seeking both convenience and value. Operational execution focuses on seamless logistics and customer retention, supporting recurring revenue and reducing churn.

---

## Business Impact

The subscription discount model drives recurring revenue by locking in regular purchases, reduces customer churn through convenience and savings, and strengthens brand loyalty. It leverages operational efficiencies in logistics and digital engagement to maximize retention and lifetime value.
