# Marketing Brief

---

## Target Persona

Busy families and working professionals who regularly consume breakfast cereals and value convenience, reliability, and cost savings in their grocery routines.

---

## Value Proposition

General Mills offers a subscription-based cereal delivery service, providing customers with scheduled, doorstep deliveries of their favorite cereals at a locked-in price. This model eliminates the hassle of frequent store visits and ensures a consistent breakfast supply. By focusing on cost-effective logistics and operational efficiency, the program delivers convenience without relying on traditional discounts, aligning with the 'No Discounts' strategy while still driving retention and recurring revenue.

---

## Business Impact

This subscription model creates a predictable revenue stream, reduces customer churn, and strengthens brand loyalty by embedding General Mills cereals into daily routines. Operational focus on cost efficiency supports margin goals while enhancing customer lifetime value.
