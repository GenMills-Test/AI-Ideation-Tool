# Marketing Brief

---

## Target Persona

Older adults with limited hand strength or dexterity who value independent snacking and freshness in packaged cookies.

---

## Value Proposition

General Mills introduces easy-open resealable cookie packaging featuring large, ergonomic tabs and closures designed for minimal strength or dexterity. This packaging ensures cookies remain fresh and accessible, directly addressing the needs of older adults who may struggle with traditional packs. The solution supports cost-effective accessibility improvements, aligns with operational feasibility, and delivers a tangible benefit by reducing frustration and waste for the target audience.

---

## Business Impact

This packaging innovation enhances product accessibility, driving incremental demand among older adults and caregivers. It supports brand loyalty, reduces product returns due to damaged packs, and aligns with cost-effective operational goals by leveraging moderate redesign for strong accessibility gains.
