# Marketing Brief

---

## Target Persona

Eco-conscious Gen Z consumers who prioritize both environmental responsibility and personal wellness, seeking indulgent treats that align with their values and lifestyle choices.

---

## Value Proposition

General Mills introduces a chocolate line produced with verified carbon-neutral methods, ensuring every bar supports a healthier planet. The product features responsibly sourced ingredients, eco-friendly packaging, and clear carbon-neutral certification. This chocolate not only satisfies cravings but also empowers Gen Z consumers to make sustainable choices without compromising on taste or quality. The execution leverages transparent supply chain practices and visible environmental credentials, making it easy for consumers to identify and trust the product’s impact. The approach is grounded in operational feasibility and cost-conscious certification, ensuring scalability and authenticity.

---

## Business Impact

This initiative drives incremental demand among Gen Z by aligning with their sustainability values, enhancing brand equity, and opening new distribution opportunities in eco-focused retail channels. It supports long-term loyalty and positions General Mills as a leader in ethical snacking.
