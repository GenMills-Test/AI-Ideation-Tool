# Marketing Brief

---

## Target Persona

Value-driven grocery shoppers who actively seek savings and digital convenience, especially families and individuals who frequently purchase General Mills products and are comfortable using mobile apps for in-store or online shopping

---

## Value Proposition

General Mills introduces a mobile platform that delivers digital coupons and instant cashback for purchases across its product portfolio. Users can easily browse, activate, and redeem offers directly from their smartphones, whether shopping in-store or online. The platform is designed to be retail-compliant and leverages public participation to maximize reach and engagement. By streamlining the savings process and making rewards instantly accessible, the solution encourages both trial and repeat purchases, supporting the goal of 100% revenue growth through scalable, tech-enabled consumer engagement

---

## Business Impact

This platform directly drives incremental revenue by incentivizing trial and repeat purchases, increasing basket size, and fostering brand loyalty. The retail-compliant, scalable approach ensures broad adoption, amplifying demand and accelerating revenue growth through digital-first consumer engagement
