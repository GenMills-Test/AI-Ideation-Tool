# Marketing Brief

---

## Target Persona

Digitally savvy, price-sensitive Gen Z online grocery shoppers who frequently compare prices and seek the best deals on branded food products during their e-commerce journeys.

---

## Value Proposition

General Mills introduces a dynamic price-matching tool integrated into online carts, designed to automatically match or beat competitor prices for its products at checkout. This tool operates seamlessly in the background, scanning competitor listings in real time and adjusting prices to ensure Gen Z shoppers always receive the lowest available price. The solution is built for transparency and ease, requiring no manual input from users. It supports General Mills’ goal of capturing value-driven consumers, leveraging moderate tech investment for ongoing competitive monitoring and price agility.

---

## Business Impact

This tool drives incremental sales by converting price-sensitive shoppers who might otherwise abandon carts for lower-priced competitors. It enhances brand loyalty, increases online conversion rates, and positions General Mills as a leader in digital pricing innovation.
