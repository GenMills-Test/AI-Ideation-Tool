# Marketing Brief

---

## Target Persona

Social hosts and event planners seeking interactive, customizable snack solutions for group gatherings, including family get-togethers, parties, and casual celebrations. Focused on convenience, creativity, and multi-brand variety for diverse guest preferences.

---

## Value Proposition

General Mills’ Build-Your-Own Snack Boards kit delivers a curated assortment of cereals, snack mixes, and dips, enabling guests to assemble personalized snack boards at social events. Each kit is designed for easy setup and maximum creativity, encouraging group engagement and hands-on participation. The multi-brand friendly approach ensures a wide variety of flavors and textures, catering to different tastes and dietary needs. Packaging is optimized for freshness and visual appeal, supporting seamless integration into any gathering. This solution empowers hosts to offer a memorable, interactive food experience while minimizing prep time and maximizing guest satisfaction.

---

## Business Impact

Drives incremental sales through bundled product offerings, increases brand exposure at social occasions, and strengthens multi-brand portfolio relevance. Enhances consumer engagement and loyalty by positioning General Mills as the go-to choice for creative, shareable snacking experiences.
