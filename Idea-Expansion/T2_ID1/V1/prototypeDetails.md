# Marketing Brief

---

## Target Persona

Families and snack enthusiasts seeking festive, limited-edition treats for holiday gatherings and seasonal celebrations.

---

## Value Proposition

The Gingerbread Snack Mix blends Chex Mix and cereals with gingerbread spices, mini marshmallows, and festive sprinkles to create a craveable, holiday-exclusive snack. Designed for multi-brand flexibility, this mix leverages existing production lines for efficient rollout. The product delivers a unique seasonal flavor experience, visually appealing for holiday tables and gatherings, and is positioned to drive trial and excitement among consumers looking for new, limited-time offerings. The execution is operationally simple, cost-effective, and aligns with the goal of boosting seasonal engagement.

---

## Business Impact

This holiday-exclusive mix is expected to drive incremental sales and brand engagement by capitalizing on seasonal demand, increasing trial, and leveraging multi-brand synergy with minimal operational disruption.
