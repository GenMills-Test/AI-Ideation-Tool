# Marketing Brief

---

## Target Persona

Health-conscious grocery shoppers and families seeking convenient, low-sugar snack options in mainstream retail environments.

---

## Value Proposition

General Mills partners with retailers to create dedicated low-sugar snack aisles, featuring a curated selection of our branded products. These aisles are supported by clear in-store signage and educational materials, making it easy for shoppers to identify and choose healthier snack alternatives. The approach is cost-effective, leverages existing retail partnerships, and ensures that health-focused consumers can quickly find suitable options without searching through standard aisles. Execution is streamlined for retailers, requiring minimal disruption while maximizing product visibility and shopper engagement.

---

## Business Impact

This initiative drives incremental sales by capturing the growing demand for low-sugar snacks, increases brand visibility in high-traffic retail locations, and strengthens retailer partnerships. It supports revenue growth through targeted demand generation and improved shopper retention.
