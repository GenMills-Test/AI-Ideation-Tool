# Marketing Brief

---

## Target Persona

Adventurous young adults and snack enthusiasts in urban India who seek bold, global flavors and enjoy experimenting with new, street food-inspired snacks at home or on the go.

---

## Value Proposition

Tikka Masala Bugles offer a unique snacking experience by combining the familiar crunch of Bugles with a tangy, aromatic tikka masala seasoning. This fusion delivers the excitement of Indian street food in a convenient, ready-to-eat format. Designed for adventurous snackers, the product leverages scalable production and moderate formulation costs, ensuring accessibility and consistent quality. The bold flavor profile stands out on shelves and appeals to consumers looking for something new, while the familiar Bugles shape ensures instant recognition and ease of consumption.

---

## Business Impact

Tikka Masala Bugles are positioned to drive incremental sales by attracting flavor-seeking consumers, expanding the Bugles portfolio into global fusion territory, and increasing brand relevance among younger, trend-driven snackers. This innovation supports both trial and repeat purchase through differentiated taste and strong shelf appeal.
