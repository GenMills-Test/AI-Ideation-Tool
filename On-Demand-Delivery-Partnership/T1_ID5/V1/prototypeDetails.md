# Marketing Brief

---

## Target Persona

Digitally savvy, convenience-focused snackers aged 18–35 who frequently use delivery apps for late-night or impulse snacking, value personalization, and are motivated by loyalty rewards and seamless digital experiences.

---

## Value Proposition

General Mills introduces microwavable, single-serve snack packs from its leading brands, available exclusively through partnered delivery apps. AI-driven recommendations personalize snack suggestions based on user behavior, time, and weather, while an integrated loyalty program enables consumers to earn and redeem points directly within the app. This ecosystem is designed for digital natives seeking instant gratification, driving repeat engagement and maximizing impulse purchases. Execution leverages exclusive delivery partnerships and limited-time, AI-curated snack bundles, all while navigating packaging innovation, tech integration, and data-sharing constraints.

---

## Business Impact

This initiative boosts incremental snack sales by capturing late-night and impulse demand, increases delivery channel penetration, and drives loyalty program participation. Operational value is realized through digital engagement, repeat purchase incentives, and strengthened brand presence in the evolving digital snacking landscape.
